About Mini Blog

Welcome to Mini Blog, a simple and elegant blogging platform built with Django.

Features

  • Create, read, update, and delete blog posts
  • Schedule posts for future publication
  • Set expiration dates for posts
  • User authentication and authorization
  • Responsive design with Bootstrap
  • Admin panel for content management

How to Use

  1. Register or login to your account
  2. Create new posts using the "New Post" button
  3. Set publication and expiration dates for your posts
  4. View and manage your posts from your profile
